Hector Miles
Hello! My name is Hector Miles, and I'm the founder of Likwel.
Founder CEO.
My story began in 2010, when I finished my master's degree, before going on to finish my postgraduate studies in computer engineering. Since 2015, I've been actively working with corporate clients in the business industry. My ideology is that I always put partners first, which is why my business has been actively developing and growing since 2015. The idea of creating my own business started in 2015 when I learned that my friends were looking for a means to optimize their business assets. For them, as for any entrepreneur, it was important to reduce costs and save money internally. At that time, I was an experienced computer science engineer, and I offered to create a well-thought-out and functioning software for their businesses. Today, they are one of the key business partners of my company. If you or your company is using a CRM system, then my company is getting a % of the profits from you using it. All this was made possible through the unique software code that I've written; code that has become an important component of most CRM systems responsible for cost control.

In 2017, with the growing popularity of the crypto and blockchain technology, I began to expand my horizons. I set my sights on blockchain, and that same year at the European Blockchain Convention in Barcelona, I met two talented individuals named Alice Moore and Marvin McKenney. Later on, we formed a team and started working on a common goal, namely to study blockchain technology and to start optimizing IT and blockchain related companies. We started gathering an audience and analyzing it. After some time, we identified several parameters that were similar to each other. The most basic common component of our audience was saving time for profit. We started planning a product that would be useful for business people, specifically those who spend most of their time at the workplace. Creating and maintaining a successful business is a huge time and energy sink, and that's no secret. The brainchild of every businessperson comes first for them, but they often lack the time to truly understand that. From the experience I've gathered with CRM systems, exploring new crypto spaces, and teaming up with Alice Moore and Marvin McKenney on a visionary team, I made the decision to found Likwel in 2022.

Likwel was created so that people too busy running their business could entrust experts with a part of the work and get help in making more money. My company's aim is to reduce the time you spend to get more profits. Time optimization is my specialty. I've come a long way from the mere idea to finally realizing my big dream over several years, a dream of creating a company where employees would feel comfortable working, where new ideas are always welcome, and most importantly, where we can do what we like and benefit the lives of our customers. I'm confident that Likwel will eventually conquer new heights, with you being there to see it all, as that's what we're working for!
